## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Why can't I see certain content?

Some content may be:
- Expired (auto-purged for privacy)
- Restricted to higher trust levels
- Hidden by the poster

### Why is my message showing as "failed"?

This usually means:
- No internet connection
- Recipient's keys haven't synced
- Try refreshing and resending

### How do I delete my account?

Contact an admin through the Help section. Account deletion is permanent and removes all your data.

### Can admins read my messages?

No! Messages are end-to-end encrypted. Only you and the recipient can read them.

### How do trust levels work?

Trust levels are assigned by admins based on:
- How long you've been a member
- Community participation
- Vouches from other members

Higher trust levels may unlock additional features.
